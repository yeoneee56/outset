Q&A FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • HOW OFTEN CAN I USE THIS?

    Although this scrub is gentle enough to use daily, we recommend using this 2-3x a week for best results.

  • WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS?

    Exfoliation is such a important part of a healthy skincare routine! This clean formula helps gently exfoliate the skin without irritation. Ideal for dull or lackluster skin, it helps to improve skin texture and radiance for a rejuvenated appearance.

  • HOW CAN I WORK THIS INTO MY DAILY ROUTINE?

    Use this on cleansed, makeup-free skin for best results. Scarlett and Kate both use this in the morning when they shower for a quick 1 minute facial. At night, we would recommend using the Gentle Micellar Antioxidant Cleanser to cleanse and remove makeup first, then use the Micro Polish.
