FAQS

  • HOW DO SUBSCRIPTIONS WORK?

    The Outset's Subscription service is the easiest way to get your products delivered on your schedule, making sure you never run out of the essentials. Select the product that you would like to purchase, select 'Subscribe and Save' along with your desired delivery frequency, and checkout. From there, we'll make sure your products are reordered on time. For a limited time, we're offering 20% off free shipping on your first subscription order, then 10% off free shipping on all following subscription orders. You can adjust, skip or cancel at any time.

  • WHICH FREQUENCY SHOULD I CHOOSE?

    If you use the recommended amount of any of our products, the full size should last you around 45 days. However, you have the option of selecting 60, 75 of 90 days based on your personal use of the products.

  • CAN I CANCEL MY SUBSCRIPTION AT ANY TIME?

    Yes, you can choose to skip a delivery at any time. Navigate to your subscription account and go to 'upcoming orders.' From there, select the subscription order you would like to cancel and select the option at the bottom of the page. If there is more than one product in your subscription, you must select the individual item you would like to cancel.

  • CAN I SKIP A DELIVERY?

    Yes, you can choose to skip a delivery at any time. Navigate to your subscription account and go to 'upcoming orders.' From there, click into the first upcoming order, then click 'skip this order' at the bottom of the page.

  • CAN I CHANGE THE DELIVERY FREQUENCY OF MY SUBSCRIPTION?

    Yes, you can change the frequency of your subscription by clicking into 'subscriptions,' then updating the order frequency on that page.

  • CAN I ADD OTHER PRODUCTS ONTO MY SUBSCRIPTION?

    Yes, you can add either a one time product or another subscription to an existing order. Navigate to 'subscriptions' and click 'add a product.' From there, you can select a product to add as either a recurring order with 10% off, or one time order.

  • CAN I UPDATE MY SHIPPING OR BILLING ADDRESS ON MY ORDER?

    You can update your shipping address by clicking into 'shipping addresses' then adding your shipping address and clicking next. You must use an associated payment with the new address. If you would like to add a new payment method and billing address, you will need to place a new order.

  • HOW FAR IN ADVANCE DO I NEED TO MAKE ADJUSTMENTS TO MY SUBSCRIPTION?

    Any adjustment to your subscription order must be made by 5PM ET the day before your order is set to renew.

  • CAN I USE A DISCOUNT CODE ON MY SUBSCRIPTION ORDER?

    At this time, we do not allow other discounts to be used with a subscription order, but we do offer 10% off all recurring orders!

  • WHEN WILL MY CARD BE CHARGED?

    You will be charged for your subscription on the renewal date of your next order.
