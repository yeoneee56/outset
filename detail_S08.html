Q&A FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • HOW OFTEN CAN I USE THIS?

    We recommend using our Purifying Clay Mask one to two times per week.

  • WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS?

    Helps to draw out impurities, absorbs excess sebum and regulate oil production. Reduces shine and makes the skin more even-looking. Pores look smaller and the overall complexion is more uniform.

  • HOW CAN I WORK THIS INTO MY DAILY ROUTINE?

    Gently cleanse the skin before each use. Apply mask and let sit for 10 minutes or until fully dry. Rinse off, pat dry and follow with The Outset Prep Serum and Daily Moisturizer.

  • WHAT OTHER PRODUCTS WORK BEST WITH THIS?

    The Outset Daily Moisturizer and Night Cream complement this mask wonderfully. Add a few drops of The Outset Ultralight Moisture-Boosting Oil for an extra dose of nourishment.
