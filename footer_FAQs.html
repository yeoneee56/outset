FAQS

Need more help? Reach out to us with any questions you may have at help@theoutset.com. Our team will get back to you within 2 business days.

ORDERS & PAYMENT

  • HOW QUICKLY IS MY ORDER PROCESSED?

    Orders are typically processed within 48-hours of receipt during normal business hours, and orders received before 12pm EST typically process and ship within the same day. Any order received after the cutoff time will be processed the following business day. Please note that purchases are subject to bank authorization prior to processing.

  • HOW DO I CHECK ON AN ORDER I HAVE PLACED?

    Once your order is placed, you will receive an email with the details of your purchase. When your order has been fulfilled, you will receive an email shipment confirmation with tracking information. You can also view the status of an order by logging in to your account and selecting Order History. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact our customer service at help@theoutset.com and have your order number available.

  • CAN I USE MULTIPLE PROMO CODES AT CHECKOUT?

    You can only use one promo code at checkout.

  • I QUALIFIED FOR FREE SHIPPING UNTIL I ENTERED A PROMO CODE. WHY AM I BEING CHARGED A SHIPPING FEE?

    All orders over $50 are eligible for free standard shipping. If your cart total is brought below $50 after you apply a promo code, you will be charged for the shipping cost.

  • DO YOU CHARGE SALES TAX?

    Yes, we are required by law to collect state sales tax.

  • WHAT FORMS OF PAYMENT DO YOU ACCEPT?

    We accept Visa, Mastercard, Discover, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, Shop Pay, Google Pay, and Afterpay. Unfortunately, we cannot accept CODs, personal checks, money orders, layaway, or credit cards with billing address outside the United States.

  • WHAT CAN I DO IF MY PAYMENT IS DECLINED?

    If your payment did not go through, first check that your card's billing address and security code match what you have entered in our system.

  • WHY WAS MY ORDER CANCELLED?

    Occasionally, orders or parts of an order are cancelled by our system for various reasons. Your item(s) may not be available, or there may be an issue in processing your shipping, billing or payment information. If your order is cancelled, you will receive an email notice alerting you to the cancellation. You will not be billed for any cancelled items, however please allow several business days for the refund to process. If you are interested in placing a new order or if you have questions about a cancelled order, please contact us. Please note: All purchases are subject to bank authorization prior to processing. Only authorized purchases may be processed and shipped.

  • IS THIS SITE SAFE AND SECURE?

    At The Outset, we understand how important your online security is. We implement various security measures in accordance with industry standards to protect your personal information, both online and offline. The information you provide will not be given to any third party. You can view our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use, or if you have any questions, please contact us at privacy@theoutset.com.

  • WHERE CAN I BUY THE OUTSET PRODUCTS?

    You can buy our products on our website or in select Sephora stores.

SHIPPING & RETURNS

  • WHERE DOES THE OUTSET SHIP PRODUCT?

    The Outset accepts orders that are billed and shipped to locations in the United States, U.S. Territories, P.O. Boxes, and APO/FPO Addresses. Orders being sent to P.O. Boxes, APO/FPO addresses, Alaska, or Hawaii must be shipped via the United States Postal Service and cannot be shipped via express shipping.

  • WHAT SHIPPING OPTIONS DO YOU OFFER AND WHAT DO THEY COST?

    The Outset offers Free Ground Shipping. We ship to locations in the United States, U.S. Territories, P.O. Boxes, and APO/FPO Addresses. Once it ships, your order should arrive in 5-7 days when shipping to the continental U.S. We also offer UPS express shipping for $15 which typically arrives in 1-2 business days. Orders being sent to P.O. Boxes, APO/FPO addresses, Alaska, or Hawaii must be shipped via the United States Postal Service and cannot be shipped via express shipping.

  • WHAT IS YOUR RETURN POLICY?

    We happily offer free returns on purchases from TheOutset.com within 30 days of the shipment date. At The Outset, we are committed to making products that are proven to work and we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not totally satisfied with your purchase, please send an email to returns@theoutset.com to begin the return process. Please Note: We are only able to assist with orders placed on the TheOutset.com. We do not accept returns from APO/FPO addresses.

  • HOW DO I PLACE A RETURN OR EXCHANGE?

    In order to initiate a return or exchange, contact us at help@theoutset.com Please note: We cannot accept an item that has been opened, unless the product was damaged or defective upon receipt. In the case of a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, please contact us at help@theoutset.com and our team will be happy to assist you.

  • WHAT SHOULD I DO IF I RECEIVE THE WRONG PRODUCT?

    If you receive the wrong product, please reach out to our customer support team at help@theoutset.com.

  • WHAT SHOULD I DO IF MY ORDER IS EITHER MISSING A PRODUCT, OR THE PRODUCT ARRIVED DAMAGED?

    Please reach out to our customer support team at help@theoutset.com and we'll be happy to help.

SUBSCRIPTION

  • HOW DO SUBSCRIPTIONS WORK?

    The Outset's Subscription service is the easiest way to get your products delivered on your schedule, making sure you never run out of the essentials. Select the product that you would like to purchase, select 'Subscribe and Save' along with your desired delivery frequency, and checkout. From there, we'll make sure your products are reordered on time. For a limited time, we're offering 20% off free shipping on your first subscription order, then 10% off free shipping on all following subscription orders. You can adjust, skip or cancel at any time.

  • WHICH FREQUENCY SHOULD I CHOOSE?

    If you use the recommended amount of any of our products, the full size should last you around 45 days. However, you have the option of selecting 60, 75 of 90 days based on your personal use of the products.

  • CAN I CANCEL MY SUBSCRIPTION AT ANY TIME?

    Yes, you can choose to skip a delivery at any time. Navigate to your subscription account and go to 'upcoming orders.' From there, select the subscription order you would like to cancel and select the option at the bottom of the page. If there is more than one product in your subscription, you must select the individual item you would like to cancel.

  • CAN I SKIP A DELIVERY?

    Yes, you can choose to skip a delivery at any time. Navigate to your subscription account and go to 'upcoming orders.' From there, click into the first upcoming order, then click 'skip this order' at the bottom of the page.

  • CAN I CHANGE THE DELIVERY FREQUENCY OF MY SUBSCRIPTION?

    Yes, you can change the frequency of your subscription by clicking into 'subscriptions,' then updating the order frequency on that page.

  • CAN I ADD OTHER PRODUCTS ONTO MY SUBSCRIPTION?

    Yes, you can add either a one time product or another subscription to an existing order. Navigate to 'subscriptions' and click 'add a product.' From there, you can select a product to add as either a recurring order with 10% off, or one time order.

  • CAN I UPDATE MY SHIPPING OR BILLING ADDRESS ON MY ORDER?

    You can update your shipping address by clicking into 'shipping addresses' then adding your shipping address and clicking next. You must use an associated payment with the new address. If you would like to add a new payment method and billing address, you will need to place a new order.

  • HOW FAR IN ADVANCE DO I NEED TO MAKE ADJUSTMENTS TO MY SUBSCRIPTION?

    Any adjustment to your subscription order must be made by 5PM ET the day before your order is set to renew.

  • CAN I USE A DISCOUNT CODE ON MY SUBSCRIPTION ORDER?

    At this time, we do not allow other discounts to be used with a subscription order, but we do offer 10% off all recurring orders!

  • WHEN WILL MY CARD BE CHARGED?

    WHEN WILL MY CARD BE CHARGED? You will be charged for your subscription on the renewal date of your next order.

PRODUCT

  • HOW DO YOUR PRODUCTS DIFFER FROM OTHER SKINCARE BRANDS?

    The Outset breaks free from overcomplicated, trendy skin routines. We help you find freedom in the uncomplicated with reliable and effective products that elevate your everday. Our consciously clean formulas are gentle enough to use every day and nourish today's skin to preserve it for the future, all without harsh ingredients that strip the skin. Inside every product is our signature Hyaluroset(TM) Complex, a botanic alternative to hyaluronic acid sourced from the seeds of the Cassia flower. All of The Outset's products are allergy and dermatologist tested, vegan, cruelty free, and free of sulfates, phthalates and parabens, so we're perfect for all skin types, including sensitive.

  • WHAT SKIN TYPES ARE THE OUTSET'S PRODUCTS SUITABLE FOR?

    Our products are suitable for all skin types, including sensitive. At The Outset, it is important for us to make our products as universal as possible by formulating without common ingredients of concern that may cause irritation, included fragrance, gluten and nut allergens, parabens and phthalates.

  • ARE THE PRODUCTS ALL-NATURAL?

    We formulate with botanical extracts and oils combined with safe synthetics to help minimize our impact on the planet.

  • DO YOU TEST ON ANIMALS?

    The Outset is leaping bunny certified and does not test our products or the ingredients used in our products on animals.

  • ARE YOUR PRODUCTS VEGAN AND GLUTEN-FREE?

    Our products are all vegan and gluten free.

  • ARE YOUR PRODUCTS NON-COMEDOGENIC?

    Yes, most of our products are clinically tested to be non-comedogenic. We have not conducted testing on the the Eye Cream since it is a very targeted formula.

  • DOES THE OUTSET USE PARABENS?

    All of our products are paraben free.

  • IS IT SAFE TO USE YOUR PRODUCTS WHILE I AM PREGNANT/NURSING?

    Although our products are gentle and do not contain any known contraindications, please always consult your doctor if you have any concerns.

  • DOES THE OUTSET USE PRESERVATIVES?

    Yes, we use safe preservatives to ensure efficacy and shelf stability.

  • IS YOUR PACKAGING RECYCLABLE?

    Our packaging comprises of a mix of post-consumer recycled material, recyclable glass and bio-resin derived from sugarcane where possible. We are constantly looking for new ways to incorporate more recyclable and post consumer materials into our packaging that won't compromise product efficacy or safety. Learn more about our packaging here.

  • HOW LONG SHOULD YOUR PRODUCTS LAST?

    Unopened products are good for 2 years. Keep your products in a clean, dry place. After opening, we suggest to use your products within 6 months.

  • HOW CAN I FIND OUT MORE ABOUT A PRODUCT?

    You can learn more about our products on their individual product pages. If you have any other questions, you can contact us at help@theoutset.com.

  • THE PRODUCT I WANT IS OUT OF STOCK, WILL IT BE AVAILABLE AGAIN?

    If an item selected is temporarily out of stock at the time of your order, you will not be able to add the item to your cart. By signing up for our emails, you'll be the first to know when an item you're looking for will be back in stock.

  • DO YOU SEND SAMPLES?

    If you create an account, you will be the first to hear about special offers for free samples.

HYALUROSET™ COMPLEX

  • WHAT IS HYALUROSET™ COMPLEX?

    Sourced from the seeds of the Cassia flower, Hyaluroset™ Complex is our signature ingredient inside every product. It acts as a botanical alternative to hyaluronic acid to nourish, plump and smooth the skin.

  • WHAT IS HYALUROSET™ COMPLEX A SUBSTITUTE FOR?

    Hyaluroset™ Complex acts as botanical alternative to hyaluronic acid. Both are high-molecular weight polysaccharide which acts like a magnet to bind moisture to the skin.

  • WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS OF HYALUROSET™ COMPLEX?

    Hyaluroset™ Complex is our signature botanic alternative to hyaluronic acid that is derived from the seeds of the Cassia flower that delivers intense and long-lasting moisture to nourish, plump and smooth.

BEHIND THE BRAND

  • DOES SCARLETT JOHANSSON OWN THE OUTSET?

    Scarlett Johansson is a co-founder of The Outset. Scarlett wanted to create and represent a brand that felt true to her, and after teaming up with Kate Foster, they worked together to bring the brand to life.

  • WHERE DOES THE BRAND NAME COME FROM?

    The Outset literally means ‘from the start’, or ‘at the beginning’. Our founders Scarlett and Kate created this brand for anyone who wants to begin again, no matter where they are in their skin journey.

  • DOES SCARLETT USE THE PRODUCTS?

    Yes! The line is inspired by Scarlett's consistent routine of cleansing, prepping and moisturizing to keep her skin healthy, balanced and camera-ready. Scarlett incorporates all of The Outset's product into her skincare routine, and her favorite product is the Restorative Niacinamide Night Cream!
