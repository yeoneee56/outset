Shipping & Return Policy

  • RETURNS

    We happily offer free returns on purchases from TheOutset.com within 30 days of the shipment date. At The Outset, we are committed to making products that are proven to work and we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not totally satisfied with your purchase, please send an email to returns@theoutset.com to begin the return process.

    We are only able to assist with orders placed on the TheOutset.com. We do not accept returns from APO/FPO addresses.

  • SHIPPING

    The Outset offers Free Ground Shipping. We ship to locations in the United States, U.S. Territories, P.O. Boxes, and APO/FPO Addresses.

    Orders being sent to P.O. Boxes, APO/FPO addresses, Alaska, or Hawaii must be shipped via the United States Postal Service and cannot be shipped via express shipping.

    Free shipping

    We offer free standard shipping for all U.S. orders. Free shipping is based on your order total after promotional discounts have been applied and before shipping charges, taxes, etc.

    Shipping rates

    Shipping is free for all U.S. orders. UPS express shipping is $15.

    Where we ship

    We currently ship to locations within the domestic United States. Unfortunately, we do not currently ship to international locations. Please don't hesitate to contact us at with any questions

    Cancellation Policy

    Some items in our store may be offered to you as a subscription. This cancellation policy lays out how you can change or cancel these kinds of purchases.

    Subscriptions

    When you purchase a subscription you'll receive repeat deliveries. These are based on the subscription duration and frequency that you select. There is no commitment and no minimum to buy. Your payment details will be stored securely and you'll be charged for each of these deliveries until you cancel your subscription, unless you choose to pay in advance. Some subscriptions may auto-renew at the end of their duration. If you don't want to renew a subscription you can cancel it. If you want to cancel or change your subscription, you can do it at any time. Your order confirmation emails have links to your order. In addition, you may cancel your subscription by chatting with customer service agents by visiting our customer service page. You can manage your subscription from there. See our returns policy for more details on returns and refunds.
