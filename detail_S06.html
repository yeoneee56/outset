Q&A FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • WHAT SKIN TYPE IS BEST SUITED FOR THIS PRODUCT?

    Our intensely moisturizing night cream is tested on sensitive skin and is non-comedogenic, so it's appropriate for all skin types.

  • WHAT DOES NIACINAMIDE DO FOR THE SKIN?

    Niacinamide helps to boost cell turnover, brighten and improve the look of skin's texture for a youthful appearance.

  • WHAT IS THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN THE DAY AND NIGHT MOISTURIZER?

    Your skin tends to lose more hydration at night when you sleep, so our night cream is specifically formulated to be richer and more nourishing to support the skin's needs while you sleep.

  • DOES IT CONTAIN ANY FRAGRANCES OR PERFUMES?

    No, this product is fragrance-free.
