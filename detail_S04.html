Q&A FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • WHAT SKIN TYPE IS BEST SUITED FOR THIS PRODUCT?

    This formula is suitable for all skin types, including sensitive.

  • WHAT OTHER PRODUCTS PAIR WELL WITH THIS ONE?

    For best results, use this in combination with the Gentle Micellar Antioxidant Cleanser and Firming Vegan Collagen Prep serum in the Daily Essentials Regimen. These three steps are all you need for healthy, hydrated and balanced skin.

  • WILL THIS CLOG MY PORES?

    No, our Nourishing Squalane Daily Moisturizer is non-comedogenic, so it won't clog your pores.

  • HOW OFTEN CAN I USE THE DAILY MOISTURIZER?

    We recommend using this moisturizer twice a day, morning and night. If you have our Restorative Niacinamide Night Cream, you can use this in your morning routine and the night cream in your evening routine.
